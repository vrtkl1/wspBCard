Project Analysis

Orlando Tropical Transport

Site Intent

Objective:

The objective of the website redesign of Orlando Tropical Transport is to create an organized information architecture in partnership with a more focused user experience. By condensing the amount of content and creating a new navigation structure, the site will be more effective at accomplishing two goals: having users fill out an online reservation form, or call the company directly to arrange for services.

Competitive Analysis

Overview:

Orlando Tropical Transport has a large number of competitors. By searching for keywords related to the site, the results show that there are many other sites that rank higher in the search engines. There was no audience information available on Alexa.com at the time of this analysis.

Currently the site uses keywords that are not effective and are not driving visitors to the site. By using more focused keywords and updating content on the site to be more relevant to the keywords, Orlando Tropical Transport would see an improvement in search results and traffic.

Target Audience

Overview:

The target audience for the site is extremely broad. Both male and female, single, married, families, and business people are all potential customers. Some focused target audiences include customers that require transportation to and from the area airports and cruise ship terminals, and to and from the local tourist attractions. There is also a large target audience of customers who require general transportation services such as taxi pick-up and drop-off at business and residential locations throughout the area.
